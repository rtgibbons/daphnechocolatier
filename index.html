Our Passion

Thank you for joining me and sharing my passion for dark chocolate. We are a boutique company located in Southern California. Each of our chocolates is carefully crafted by hand. We use only the highest-quality nuts and dry fruit ingredients. We adhere to the highest quality standards while maintaining simplicity and elegance.

We use several different varieties of chocolate. Blending them as necessary so that each of our decadent pieces has the best balance of both sweet and rich flavors, creating a complex and sophisticated taste.

Our Specialty

Our belief in the joy of dark chocolate has inspired our signature pieces. Tastes and texture is the playground where our ideas melt into the reality of our signature chocolates. Pieces such as:

  • Orange cranberry walnut cluster covered in dark chocolate.
  • Crystal ginger dipped in deep rich dark chocolate topped with a walnut.
  • Almond brittle coated in dark chocolate sprinkled with almonds.
  • Date dip in dark chocolate rolled in walnuts.
  • Peanut butter and dark chocolate.

We welcome you to taste and enjoy our artistic confections.
